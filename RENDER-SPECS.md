# Render Specs — 1win Deck (for the image-generation pass)

Shared rules across ALL concepts:
- **Balloon branding = a 1win FLAG / logo panel on the balloon** (Stake-style), NOT a banner wrapping the whole balloon.
- **Rigs are suspended from the BASKET** on long thin cables, hanging well below the balloon — not right under it.
- **Minimal, thin, deliverable-looking rigs** — light steel truss, no thick/industrial boxes, no light bars.
- 1win logo on the rig surface + all creators in 1win merch.
- Cinematic, photorealistic, golden hour, sea of clouds + mountains. No text/watermarks.
