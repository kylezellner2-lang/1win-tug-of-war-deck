04 — How It Works

Engineered for real. Built to go viral.

01
The Rig

A single 1win balloon suspends two locked steel platforms on industrial cables. C-ring connectors hold them rigid for a fair pull.

02
The Tug of War

Two teams of two. One rope. An open drop zone in the middle. The first team pulled off the edge loses.

03
The Fall

The losers don't crash — they skydive. Chutes deploy, blue confetti erupts. Losing has never looked this good.
