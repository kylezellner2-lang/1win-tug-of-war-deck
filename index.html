01 — The Stunt

A tug of war, 10,000 feet up.

Two teams. Two steel platforms suspended beneath a single 1win hot air balloon. One rope stretched across an open drop zone.

The team that loses their footing doesn't just lose the match — they fall out of the sky. Parachutes deploy, confetti erupts. It's the most literal high-stakes game ever filmed.

The Rig
